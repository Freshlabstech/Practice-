Welcome to AAU Ekpoma Kofa

The Student Information and Registration Portal of Ambrose Alli University, Ekpoma, Nigeria

Kofa is part of the West African eUniversity Project (WAeUP). It is a powerful and comprehensive Student Management System which will become the single access point to educational resources and information for all AAUE students.

Kofa controls application and student registration processes and provides comprehensive and transparent information about study progress.
